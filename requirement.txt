3. Authentication & Authorization
There are 4 kinds of users.
- Developers: They are lowest level of users. They can see and update their developer profile. They can see the jobs of website and apply using their profiles.
- Employers: They are mid level users. They can see and update their company profile. They can post / manage multiple jobs in website and see the proposals of developers and send feedback to them and can see their profiles.
- Admins: They are moderators of website. Admins can mute / kick / ban developers and employers. Admins can create jobs and admins can allow / disallow job posts. The job posts of allowed by admins can be posted to the job board.
- Owner: This is the Owner of website. The Owner has all permissions and have most powerful permission to give / take level of user. Owner can make other users admins. Owner can take adminship from users. Owner can delete other admins. Owner can deliver their ownership to other admin. Once deliver ownership that owner becomes normal admin. This means only 1 owner exists in website.
